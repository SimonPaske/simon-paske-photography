Welcome to my page!

Hello!

My name is Simon. I am from Lithuania, but I love Denmark :)

I came to Denmark 10 years ago. Here I have found myself as a photographer. Even though since childhood, photography has fascinated me. At the age of 5, I made my first shot. It was nothing special, but the sound of the camera's curtain and forwarding film was like music to my ears.

In 2016 DFDS Seaways announced a competition named “Ambassador of Copenhagen for the Baltic States”. I decided. become a photographer if I'll win. I participated and I won. My photos were published on DFDS Seaways' social media channels and collaboration continued for 1.5 years. During my career, I had an opportunity to work with big companies such as Maersk, DFDS Seaways, Rekom, and UserTribe gave me good experience and confidence.

In 2022 I was top 10 finalist at "World Wide Photowalk" selected by Scott Kelby.




I love to capture natural moments, emotions, and surroundings. Everybody and everything has a story. I tell stories of people and places. “Simple is Perfect” is my motto when it comes to photography. I believe the light and the colors are the only aspects to be tampered with so that the end result reflects the original identity of the picture made.
